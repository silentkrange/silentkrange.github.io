Portfolio Management Space

This space has been established to improve organizational awareness of strategic initiatives. Here you will find information on approved projects, the project lifecycle, the project roadmap, and portfolio steering.

OBJECTIVES

I
Stabilize Bio and Launch Preprints

(i) Complete execution of plans to  stabilize Bio by June 30 and (ii) to launch preprints for PLOS content by April 30, with the goal of responsibly driving as many PLOS submissions as possible to preprints under a CC-BY license.

II
Demonstrate Innovation and Thought Leadership

Develop and implement strategies and infrastructure elements necessary to demonstrate innovation and thought leadership in open science, with the goal of rebuilding PLOS' reputation and desirability as a place to publish and create new revenue streams.

III
Improve Performance and Experience

Develop and implement strategies to deliver significant improvements in operational performance and editor/author experience.

IV
Grow Journal Revenue

Grow revenue for the journals program through implementation of strategies to (a) grow PLOS ONE submissions, output and revenue by 10% in 2019 (over Q4 2017 monthly actuals); and (b) attract quality submissions and grow quality output of the SIX collectively  by 10% in 2019.

V
Evaluate Infrastructure and Cost Base

Conduct a strategic evaluation of PLOS’s operating infrastructure and cost base, to be completed by April 30, 2018, to identify potential efficiencies with the goal of reducing operating expenses by $1m in 2019.

VI
Improve Operational Performance

Develop and implement strategies to improve PLOS’s operational performance through evaluation and redesign of processes and systems.

VII
Create a Culture of Engagement

Develop a culture at PLOS that attracts, retains and engages top talent through implementation of an employee engagement plan.

AD HOC

For critical projects that exist independent of a strategic objective
